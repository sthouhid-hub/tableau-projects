# Superstore Sales Analysis (Tableau)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes sales, profit, customer growth, and regional performance using the Superstore dataset.  
The objective is to identify key business insights and trends to support data-driven decision making.

---

## 📊 Dashboards & Insights

### 🔹 Total Sales
- Displays overall sales performance across all orders.

### 🔹 Sales by Category
- Compares sales across Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies.
- Technology contributes the highest sales.

### 🔹 Monthly Sales Trend
- Shows month-wise sales growth over time.
- Identifies seasonal patterns and growth periods.

### 🔹 Region-wise Profit
- Analyzes profit performance across regions.
- West and East regions generate the highest profit.
